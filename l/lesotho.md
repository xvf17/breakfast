# Lesotho
16 April, 2023

Basotho breakfast: makoenva. This is sort of a cross between cinnamon
rolls and puff puffs. (See [Cameroon's entry](../c/cameroon.md). We
both thought the serving looked a little large, but they went down
just fine.
