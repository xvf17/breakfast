# Ethiopia
10 July, 2022


Ethiopian breakfast: firfir and injera. Firfir is a stew made from
tomatoes and dried beef. I really enjoyed eating this, though I doubt
the authenticity of my efforts to season it. Definitely kept us going
till lunch.
